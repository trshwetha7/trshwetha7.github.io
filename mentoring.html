Machine Learning Mentor, SureStart Inc. (New York, NY)

Jul 2022 – Dec 2023

At SureStart, I mentored student teams on applied machine learning projects and helped them build strong fundamentals in problem framing, model design, evaluation, and iteration. I focused on helping teams turn ambiguous ideas into clear goals, choose practical approaches, and validate results in a way that holds up beyond a demo.

I led a four-person team that built an AI recycling app using YOLOv8 for object detection and an LLM component for user-facing guidance. I also mentored a five-person team developing a news bias detection model using BERT; the project earned third place at MIT FutureMakers. In addition, I served as a judge for the MIT FutureMakers Hackathon, where I evaluated ML projects on technical quality, feasibility, and real-world impact, and shared actionable feedback to help teams improve.
