Spendlark Money

Spendlark Money is a personal finance insights app that turns raw transaction statements into a clean monthly story of where your money went. It ingests statements and organizes transactions into understandable categories so you can see spending patterns without manual tracking. It highlights month-over-month changes, flags unusual spikes, and surfaces subscription waste that quietly adds up. It also gives forward-looking guidance so you can plan smarter for the next month. The experience is powered by machine learning for categorization and pattern detection, and an LLM layer that turns insights into simple, guided coaching.

PMS Mood Compass

PMS Mood Compass is a cycle and wellbeing tracker that goes beyond logging and helps you understand patterns over time. You track symptoms and lifestyle signals in a simple daily flow, and the app turns that history into clear insights you can actually act on. It helps you spot which habits tend to correlate with better or worse days instead of leaving you with a long list of entries. It also provides a short-term outlook based on your past tracking, so you know what to expect and can plan ahead. The intelligence is backed by machine learning that learns from your own logs and surfaces interpretable trends.

StudyBuddyAI

StudyBuddyAI is an AI education platform that teaches machine learning and modern AI in a way that builds real intuition, not just definitions. Lessons break concepts down step by step so the experience feels structured, beginner-friendly, and still technically solid. It also includes an interactive tutor so learners can ask questions while studying and get answers that stay aligned to the lesson they are on, making the experience feel closer to having a personal instructor than searching random explanations online. The tutor is LLM-backed, uses retrieval grounding to stay consistent with the course content, and incorporates a LangSmith-based evaluation workflow to trace interactions, inspect failure cases, compare prompt variants, and improve response reliability over time.

SQL Sprint

SQL Sprint is a realistic SQL interview practice platform designed to mirror real screening rounds. It uses business-style datasets across domains so the questions feel like what companies actually ask. Your queries are evaluated using real query outputs, which keeps scoring objective and repeatable. The platform also explains mistakes clearly so you learn the patterns behind the problems, not just the final answer. It includes an LLM-based helper to guide debugging and reasoning while keeping the evaluation grounded in SQL correctness.

GlowKind

GlowKind is an AI-assisted beauty product search and comparison app that makes clean shopping feel simple instead of overwhelming. You can search a product and immediately see ingredient and packaging signals explained in plain language. It highlights ingredient watch-outs, summarizes safety and sustainability signals, and makes it easier to compare products within the same category. It also recommends cleaner alternatives that are actually relevant to what you are shopping for. The scoring is backed by machine learning so product comparisons stay consistent rather than relying on vague marketing claims.
