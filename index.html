About Me

Hi,
I am Shwetha. I am a Data & Machine Learning Scientist who is genuinely obsessed with pattern recognition!! I play Quordle almost every day, not because I have an incredible vocabulary, but because I look at letters like moving patterns and constraints, and I love the feeling of narrowing the search space until the answer clicks :) That same instinct is what pulled me into data and machine learning: I enjoy finding signal in the noise, turning messy information into clear insights, and building things that people can actually use!

In my free time, I like building AI-powered applications with clean, simple user experiences, mostly because I learn best by shipping! Outside of tech, I am into photography, travel, music, sports, cafe hopping, and experimenting in the kitchen. I love the idea of baking, and I secretly dream of having a tiny cafe of my own someday!!
